Buy & Burn Process

Agicoin has a unique burn mechanism. Indeed, we have access to liquidity, this liquidity is composed of BNB and Agicoin. Every day, we will withdraw 20% of this liquidity and use it to buyback and burn Agicoin tokens. All proofs of buy and burn will of course be posted on our social networks. We hope that this mechanism will help the project to last through time compared to other similar tokens.
